The Mandate

The Centre for Regulatory Studies (CRS) is a specialized research initiative at National Law University, Delhi, engaged in deep doctrinal work on the administrative state.

Law moves slowly; technology and markets move fast. Our work exists in that friction. From the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) to the complexities of AI Governance, we provide the intellectual infrastructure for modern regulation.
