Our Mandate

The Centre for Regulatory Studies (CRS) was established to engage with the emerging regulatory landscape in India. We operate at the intersection of administrative law, constitutional theory, and economic policy.

In an era of rapid legislative changes, the Centre serves as a bridge between the judiciary, the executive, and the academy. Our mission is to provide doctrinal clarity to complex regulatory challenges ranging from Insolvency to Digital Governance.

Research Focus

Insolvency & Bankruptcy

Examining the intersection of skill-based gaming, taxation, and constitutional protections in the digital economy.

Competition Policy

Understanding market structures, antitrust enforcement, and consumer welfare in India's growing markets.
